Hi, my name is Thomas Seal and this where I bring my most creative side to life. What began as a sudden interest in typography (which probably wasn’t very sudden at all) quickly escalated into a fevered obsession with all things graphic design. This combined with my natural love of words and desire to communicate could only lead to me building a website, e voilà! I created maxwell.life to be a space for me to share my thoughts and ideas, to help me grow creatively and find my voice. This is just a humble beginning, but I’m super excited to finally have a piece of the web to call my own.
